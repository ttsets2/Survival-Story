The sun finally rose after an eternity. Your eyes puffed and bloodshot from the
tears that you had cried. With a groan you opened the hatch as sunlight filled
the pod. You didn't want do anything other but wither away but the memory of
Amy looking at you through the window with that smile on her face got you to
move.

After a quick bathroom break you began to take account of your supplies.
Which wasn't much, most of it was in the survival kit from the lifepod.
With a sigh you grabbed the tarp and tape to make a makeshift bag for water.
You had alrady begun to feel thirsty so you had to get water first.

After topping off the water tank in the life pod you began to think about
food. There was biological life here and the life pod can clean it up for you.
The question was could you capture it? The easiest option was probably the river.

With new found purpose you began to make a spear to fish in the river with.
You didn't want to, no you couldn't waste this chance that Amy has given you.
If you couldn't survive for yourself, you'd survive to tell her parents yourself.
