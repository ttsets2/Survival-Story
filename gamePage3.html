It was already dark by the time you made it back. The life pod had no lights,
and instead came equipped with infrared lights that were visible from space.
Though with the burning wreck nearby they hardly needed the lights from the
lifepod. You looked at the pillar of orange smoke. A feeling of frustration
briefly taking hold before you let it go.

It was dark inside the lifepod. A measure to save the batteries. The solar
panels were damaged on reentry. Sitting in the dark you began to wonder again
your thoughts reaching for those dark crevices that lay in your subconsious.
The faces of your friends contorted and screaming in agony within the blaze.
You try to run to them but a wall stops you. They turn to face you and in
in unison began to speak. "You ran away without us! You left us to die!"
Their screams growing in intensity with each repetition.
Suddenly they charge at you, crossing the barrier that you couldn't.
But just before they grab you with their burning hands, you wake up.

It was still night. You didn't know how much time was left till sunrise'
but you'd rather stay awake then fall asleep again. The tortured faces of
your friends still on fresh on your mind.
