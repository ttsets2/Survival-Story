You landed on the ground safely. Luckily the reactor, as per security
protocol, had already been shut off so there was no danger of radiation.
A silent vibration coursed through the caosule as it began to open.
The wall embedded screen flickered to life as it began to display information.

Atmospheric Composition: SAFE TO BREATH
Pressure: 1.4 atm
DANGER : HIGH CONCENTRATION OF CO2 (0.08%)
Gravity: 0.96 G
Signal: NONE
Location of Other Survivors: NONE FOUND
WARNING: EXTRA RATIONS MISSING!

You froze. Staring at the last two words flashing in red. Mocking you.
Quickly you struggled to get out of the seat and jumped towards the screen.
Going through every single menu, activating every single sensor, even
bringing up the last known state of the ship. But all of it mocked your efforts.
You were alone. Alone on an alien world, the last one to be left.
With a shaky step you walked towards the door and opened it.

You still had to live.
