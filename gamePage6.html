As you made your way through the forest you stopped. There was something you
could do. A voice violently popped in and yelled at you "ARE YOU FUCKING
INSANE!?!". You spoke back at the voice. "YOU GOT A BETTER IDEA!?". The voice
didn't respond. But you didn't want to do it either. Just the idea of it made
you sick but if you did. You were definitely gonna die.

The voice came back, quieter this time. "Are you sure you want to surive
like this? There's no going back if you do this." You paused, you knew the voice
was right. Not only would you change but the people around you would also
change. He'd become a monster to himself and others. But did he have a choice?

Her accursed face appeared again but you couldn't tell what she wanted this time.
Was she encouraging him forward or telling him not to. You wondered about what
she would have done if she was here instead of you. But you couldn't think.
She seemed so distant now. Either because of time or because of your hatred
and love of her. You collapsed down as the non existent tears came out once more.

After a while, you stopped. Your eyes blank with resignation. You didn't
want to think anymore. All you knew is that you didn't want to die hungry.
With dead eyes you set forth towards the wreck site.
