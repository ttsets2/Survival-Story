Unfortunately the water was murky but you couldn't be choosy
the lifepod could clean it for you. As you stood there at the bank, the
reality of your situation began to fully hit you. Everyone else was dead.
Jon, Keo, Xia, Amy, Wong, they were all gone. Your mind drifted to the moment
before the escape pod launched.

The scent of smoke, the suffocation from the lack of oxygen. Amy's face in
the port window as she smiled at you. You saw her lips move but you couldn't
make it out before the lifepod launched. She saved you, but it had cost her,
her life. Guilt hit you as you realized that she could have survived were
it not for you. Tears began to form as your emotions took over

She should have been the one to survive. Her family was waiting for her but
now they would never meet again. If at least it was you there would have been
no one else to mourn you. DON'T THINK LIKE THAT! Suddenly you snapped
out of it. Almost as if you heard Amy shout at you. Now wasn't the time to get
emotional. You could do that when you were no longer in danger.

Examining the bank of the river you found the remains of a half eaten
creature. Confirming both that a predator was nearby and that there was
life in the river. Confirming the location you began to head back to the
lifepod before night set.
