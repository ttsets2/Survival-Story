You bit your lips again. It had alraedy been 2 weeks since you landed on this
damn planet. You haven't caught a single animal yet despite the proof that it
existed. You had even tried making fish traps and yet you still failed to
catch something. You already knew that if you didn't catch something in a
few days you'd die.

Your nerves were already at the breaking point and hunger wasn't helping.
With a loud shout you threw the spear in your hand. It splashed into the water
and began to float away. You didn't care as you continued to scream and shout.
Sonn, however, your screams changed. Turning into a quiet laughter.
You considered if you should just give up. But Amy's face appeared again.
The face that had once been a source of comfort and motivation had been
corrupted and twisted into something that you hated.

Despite that it still wouldn't let you give up. You took in a deep breath
and made your way to the shore. You sat down on the bank next to the large
boulder you named Keo and had drawn a face on. With a sign you began to
talk to it.
I don't know what to do anymore Keo. I don't know how to live.
Amy's face haunts my dreams and thoughts and despite my efforts
I haven't even caught anything to eat.
You sat there in silence before getting up and making your way back to the pod.
