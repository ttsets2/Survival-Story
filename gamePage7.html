You approached the wreck site in front of you. The fires had long since
died out. All that remained was a husk of the ship. It was the first
you had been here since you escaped through the life pod. You almost
wished you could escape and run away. Put everything behind you and
and just disappear into the wilderness.

But against the potests of your consience you pushed forward. The smell was
awful, the smell of oil and metal permeated the air. Masking any other scent
that may have been present. But the worst of all was the sheer number of
cryopods strewn out across the field. As you walked past the cryopods you
recognized the name on a few of the pods.

You looked at the wreck, You wanted to check for food inside the wreck but
looking at the state of the ship. You knew it was not possible. What foor
that had survived the crash had already been lost in the ensuing fire. You
kept going through the fields until finally you came across the pod of
someone you didn't know. You couldn't bring yourself to look at their face.
You just spoke a soft sorry .

With a huff you lifted the pod and began to drag it away.
